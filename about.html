About SportsGift Trophies

A Sydney family business built on quality, care and a passion for recognising achievement.

Our Story

SportsGift Trophies is a family business owned and operated by Peter McGrath, with over 30 years of experience in the trophy and awards industry. Based in Sydney's Sutherland Shire, we've built our reputation on quality craftsmanship, personal service and genuinely affordable pricing.

We handle everything from single-piece custom awards to large bulk orders for clubs, schools and corporate events. No order is too small or too large — every customer gets the same careful attention from start to finish.

Our speedy turnaround means you're never left scrambling before presentation night. If you need it fast, just ask — we'll do everything we can to make it happen.
