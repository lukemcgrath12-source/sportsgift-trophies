About SportsGift Trophies

A Sydney family business built on quality, care and a passion for recognising achievement.

Our Story

SportsGift Trophies was founded in 2005 by a family with deep roots in Sydney's local sporting community. What started as a small engraving workshop has grown into one of Sydney's most trusted names in custom trophies, medals and awards.

We've supplied hundreds of clubs, schools, corporates and community organisations with beautifully crafted awards — from grassroots junior football to state-level championships and black-tie gala dinners.

We believe recognition matters. A well-crafted award tells someone their effort was seen and valued. That's why we put care into every order, no matter how big or small.
